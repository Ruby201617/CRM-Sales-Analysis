# CRM-Sales-Analysis

## Project Overview
An end-to-end CRM Sales Opportunities analysis project built in Power BI, examining sales performance, pipeline efficiency, customer value, and product performance. The dashboard delivers actionable insights to support revenue growth and data-driven decision-making.

## Business Objectives
To provide stakeholders with a clear understanding of how the company can improve revenue by optimizing sales performance, pipeline, efficiency, and product strategy.

## Tools and Methodology
- Power BI
- Microsoft Excel
- Dax
- Data Cleaning and Transformation


## Methodology
The analysis was conducted using a structured data analytics workflow to ensure accurate insights and actionable recommendations.

### 1. Data Collection
- Imported CRM sales opportunities data into Excel and Power BI.
- Reviewed available fields, including opportunity details, customer information, product information, sales stages, and revenue metrics.

### 2. Data Cleaning and Preparation
- Checked for missing values, duplicates, and inconsistencies.
- Standardized data formats and validated key fields.
- Created calculated columns and measures to support analysis.

### 3. Data Modeling
- Developed relationships between relevant tables.
- Created a date table to enable time-based analysis.
- Implemented DAX measures for KPIs such as Revenue, Win Rate, Opportunity Count, and Pipeline Value.

### 4. Exploratory Data Analysis
- Analyzed sales trends and performance across different dimensions.
- Investigated sales agent performance, product performance, and pipeline progression.
- Identified patterns, anomalies, and performance drivers.

### 5. Dashboard Development
- Designed interactive Power BI dashboards to visualize key metrics.
- Added filters, slicers, tooltips, and drill-down functionality to improve user experience.
- Organized visuals around four focus areas:
  - Sales Performance
  - Pipeline Analysis
  - Customer Analysis
  - Product Analysis

### 6. Insight Generation
- Interpreted trends and performance indicators.
- Identified opportunities for revenue growth and process improvement.
- Developed data-driven recommendations for stakeholders.

## Key Insights

### Revenue Concentration by Product
- GTX Pro and GTX Plus Pro are the primary revenue drivers, contributing approximately **61% of total pipeline revenue**.
- This indicates a strong reliance on these products for overall sales performance and revenue generation.

### Regional Product Performance
- GTX 500 achieves a successful win rate only in the **East region**.
- Opportunities involving GTX 500 in other regions are more likely to result in losses, suggesting regional differences in product demand or market fit.

### Industry Performance
- The **Marketing sector** records the highest win rate among all industries.
- Despite its strong conversion performance, the sector has a relatively low number of closed deals, indicating potential for further growth.

### Sales Agent Performance
- **Darcel Schlect** is the top-performing sales agent, contributing **11.53% of total pipeline revenue**.
- The agent also records the highest number of closed deals, demonstrating strong sales effectiveness and conversion capability.

### Customer Revenue Distribution
- Revenue is evenly distributed across customer accounts.
- The business is not heavily dependent on a small number of customers, reducing customer concentration risk and supporting revenue stability.

## Recommendations

### Increase Focus on High-Performing Products
- Prioritize sales and marketing efforts around GTX Pro and GTX Plus Pro.
- Explore opportunities for upselling and cross-selling to maximize revenue from these products.

### Optimize GTX 500 Sales Strategy
- Investigate the factors driving GTX 500's success in the East region.
- Reassess pricing, positioning, and sales strategies in underperforming regions to improve win rates.

### Expand Opportunities in the Marketing Sector
- Increase prospecting and lead-generation efforts within the Marketing sector.
- Leverage its high win rate to drive a greater volume of successful deals.

### Share Best Practices Across the Sales Team
- Analyze the sales approaches used by Darcel Schlect.
- Implement knowledge-sharing initiatives to help replicate successful strategies across the team.

### Maintain Strong Customer Relationships
- Continue investing in customer retention and relationship management.
- Preserve the diversified customer base to support sustainable and stable revenue growth.
